Story
I am trying to raise funds to help my uncle who recently underwent an open heart surgery at Lilavati hospital. While the surgery was successful, it had some impact on my uncle's brain which led to him getting seizures.
It's been more than 3 weeks that he is in ICCU and hospital bills have reached almost Rs 20 Lakh (2 million INR). Doctors have hope that therapy and medication will help him recover completely in one month or so.

My aunt has exhausted all their savings and is now taking loans from all corners to pay for medicines and hospital bills. Uncle's current hospital bills and post hospital expenses will cost them approx Rs 25 Lakhs (2.5 million INR).

They don't have kids or regular income so it is now upto good samaritans like us to bring this elderly couple back on track.

Thanking you in anticipation.
