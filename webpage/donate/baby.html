Story
It was the day of 4th October 2022 when a tragic accident turned a family’s life upside down. Deshik Viraj fell from the 1st floor, and this accident left him unconscious in ICU with severe Traumatic Brain Injury.
Due to severe injuries, he was rushed to Rainbow Children's Medicare Pvt Ltd Banjara Hills, Hyderabad. Even with aggressive medication, there seems to be hardly any improvement in the baby’s health. Frequent tests, scans, medication, and constant medical attention can end the poor kid's struggle. But everything comes at a price and so does Deshik Viraj’s treatment.

The family has spent nearly 10 lakhs on the baby’s treatment and ongoing medication. But the nature of his diagnosis is never-ending. It demands a treatment that could cost the family more than 5 lakh rupees.

Despite many hardships, Dhommeti Kiran and her husband raised their 1-year-old son together, and watching him fight for every breath he takes is crushing their hope. Both parents’ unemployment is resulting to be a major setback for their son’s treatment.

Their helplessness landed Dhommeti Kiran on the crowdsourcing platform, the only option left to save her dying son! “My heart trembles every time I watch my baby lying lifeless in a hospital bed. If I could ever go back in time, I would stop this accident from happening and end my son’s suffering. I cannot change the past, however, I want to do everything in my power to save my boy. He is too young and deserves a better life.

Please help me save him” To help Deshik Viraj get a speedy chance to recover the family is in dire need of 5,00,000 /- Your aid can impart a healing touch to his pain. Life is full of uncertain events and the path to recovery after an accident is usually a chaotic journey. All we can do is help ease the pain caused to the family in this journey by donating a little towards her recovery. An act of kindness can bring a huge change to someone’s life. Hence, consider lending your support to this cause and share this as much as possible among your family and friends. Please help us raise the required amount by clicking on donate button. A friend shall always be obligated to your help.
