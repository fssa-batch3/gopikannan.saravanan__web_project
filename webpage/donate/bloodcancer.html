Story
My 9-year-old daughter, Tanaya Sarkar, feel severely sick and ran a high fever. Her symptoms were consistent with that of COVID-19 so the doctors told us to get her tested for the same. To our shock, she tested positive. We were all shattered. Our little bundle of joy was suffering from a disease that we did not even know much about.
She was admitted in the hospital for 2 weeks and fortunately, she recovered from COVID-19 and we were able to get back home. We were all beyond happy that our little warrior was safe and healthy. But our celebration was premature because after 2-3 days, she again had a fever and so we rushed her to the hospital. We were all exhausted, both mentally and physically and were not ready for what came next.

The doctors ran another COVID-19 test along with some other blood tests. Though the COVID-19 result came back negative, Tanaya’s WBC count was critically low. The doctor got suspicious and ran a bone marrow test. The results confirmed his suspicion and our daughter was diagnosed with Acute lymphoblastic leukaemia. We went numb when the doctor gave us the news. We could not believe our ears. What did our 9-year-old daughter ever do to deserve this ill fate? But, for Tanaya’s sake, we quickly gathered ourselves and asked the doctor about the course of treatment. He advised Tanaya to undergo chemotherapy for 2 years and to start immediately as the cancer was fast advancing.

Her body has become extremely weak as she is still recovering from COVID-19. As we had spent all of our saving in her COVID-19 treatment so far, we borrowed money from our friends and relatives to start Tanaya’s treatment. We need Rs 20 Lakh more for the whole treatment and you are our only hope.

I am Tanaya’s father and I work in the private sector. This amount is beyond my financial capability so I need your help to save my daughter’s life. She has an elder sister who loves her dearly and she is her mother’s lifeline. If something happens to her, none of us will be able to recover from it. She is our everything.

I request you to donate however much you can towards my daughter’s treatment and help me save her life. Every bit counts.

Thank you.
